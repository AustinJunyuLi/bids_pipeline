<objective>
Normalize transition and outcome rows.

Purpose: GPT Pro showed that drop rows are too coarse and literal outcome rows
often pick the wrong actor or no date. This plan adds explicit transition
dating, uses stronger round evidence for synthetic exits, and normalizes the
literal outcome actor/date path without widening scope into later export work.

Output: additive transition-date support in the derive artifact, earlier round
dates for synthetic drops when warranted, and bidder-first literal outcome
rows.
</objective>
