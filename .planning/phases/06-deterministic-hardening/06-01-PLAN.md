<objective>
Harden the shared extract loader and canonicalize against the two schema-integrity
failures documented in the 7-deal rerun: mixed actor/event schema modes
(HARD-06) and cross-array quote_id collisions during canonicalize (HARD-01).

Purpose: This plan protects the deterministic runtime at its earliest shared
choke points so downstream stages never process partially canonicalized extracts
and canonicalize can safely upgrade quote-first artifacts that reuse quote IDs
across actors and events.

Output: a dedicated `MixedSchemaError` in `extract_artifacts.py`,
deterministic quote renumbering plus mapping audit in `canonicalize.py`, and
focused regression coverage in `tests/test_skill_extract_artifacts.py` and
`tests/test_skill_canonicalize.py`.
</objective>
